Synopsis

Unknown Soldier is a half-hour anthology series following the untold stories of American war fighters that shows how they contributed to decisive events in our history.

Each episode features a new mission, new characters, and new conflicts from the battlefield to the homefront.

Pathfinder

When a young private lands himself in the brig, he is forced to join an airborne suicide mission for a chance at going home. Together with a small band of misfits, they must work together to lead the way for D-Day behind enemy lines.

Buffalo

Download

On Christmas Day 1944, disguised German soldiers ambush an allied mountain village leaving a small group of segregated African-American soldiers surrounded. Alone without the hope of rescue, they must defend the town and make one of the biggest sacrifices of the Italian campaign.

Recorded on 6/7/19 Clip Length: 2:20

Hurtgen

Download

A group of cocky, battle worn soldiers are stranded during the bloodiest battle of the European front. Alone with a fresh replacement while facing death at everry turn, they must find their way back from enemy lines amid the horrors of the Hürtgen Forest.

Recorded on 6/7/19 Clip Length: 2:20

W.A.S.P.

Download

In 1943 two pilot sisters rush to join the Women Air Service Pilots, but only one has the experience to join. When the older sister tragically dies in a WASP crash, the other must fight harder to join and continue her legacy as one of the first female air force pilots.

Recorded on 6/7/19 Clip Length: 2:20

Corregidor

Download

In 1942, American and Phillipino troops fight side by side in their bloody surrender at Corregidor. After escaping imprisonment, one private is reassigned on a mission to take back the island from an unforgiving enemy.

Recorded on 6/7/19 Clip Length: 2:20

Go For Broke

Download

When the famed Texas Battalion is trapped behind enemy lines, The Army begrudgingly enlists the help of the 442nd — A unit entirely made up of Japanese-American soldiers. Against all odds while being treated as an expendable force, the unit must embark on the daring rescue of the "Lost Battalion."

Recorded on 6/7/19 Clip Length: 2:20
